ABOUT NACOSS FUNAAB
NACOSS which stands for Nigerian Association of Computer Science Students is one of the oldest associations in the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB). NACOSS generally is one of the present association in most universities in Nigeria as a result of it's reputable standards and dedication by her leaders. This is expressed in her slogan "Networking The World". The standards have been followed and improved upon from teams to teams in different institutions and collectively. NACOSS FUNAAB is currently led by a team of 11 known as "Team Evolution" led by Akin-Akinsanya Israel Akinola (president). Their objective is to discover the opportunities prevalent in NACOSS and to tackle her problems
